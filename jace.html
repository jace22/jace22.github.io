About Skiing

Skiing is a very popular winter sport and perhaps one of the most fun.Skiing is one of the fastest non-motorized sports on land. Skiers can ski faster than a car and this was proven by Simone Origone in 2006 that set a world speed skiing record at 156.2 miles per hour compared to the speed of road cars at 120 miles per hour.
Skiing is extremely popular in the US with 40 states having ski resorts. Vail Mountain Resort in Colorado is the nation's largest and most visited ski resort.
Around 1.5 million visitors enjoy skiing at the 5,289 acre resort annually.

The word ‘Ski’ is derived from the Norwegian word ‘skíð’ which means a split piece of wood.

Did you know that skiing was originally a form of transportation in the mountains of Europe, well before it became a sport? Skis have been used to win battles off and on screen Soldiers in mountainous regions have been using skis as a mode of wartime transport for hundreds of years. And ski troops played an important role in both the first and the second world wars.
